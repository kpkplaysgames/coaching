What you'll learn

The skills that separate top-cut from the bubble

Every session is built around the three pillars of competitive Pokémon. We diagnose your weak spots and drill them until they're instincts.

Teambuilding

Build cohesive teams with answers to the meta's biggest threats — EV spreads, item choices, and tech that wins matchups before turn one.

Metagame reads

Learn what the field is bringing and why. Anticipate leads, predict switches, and know the common lines so you're never caught off guard.

In-game decisions

The hard part: speed control, positioning, and risk assessment under pressure. We review your replays turn-by-turn to fix the costly habits.
